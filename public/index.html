Editing Services

Whether you’re self-publishing or looking for a publisher for your manuscript, even the best writers depend on an editor’s eye to be sure their writing conveys the message they intend to their target audience. But great writing depends on clarity at the sentence level—grammar, spelling, punctuation, and mechanics, yes, but also tone, conciseness, and word choice. I’m prepared to help you with any and all of these aspects of your writing as you move forward within the publishing process.
